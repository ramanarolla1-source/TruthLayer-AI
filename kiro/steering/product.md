# Product Steering: TruthLayer AI

## 1. Product Vision
To serve as the universal intelligence layer for all business communication by uncovering the "unspoken truth" behind verbal commitments. We bridge the gap between spoken transcripts and human intent.

## 2. Core Value Propositions
- **Vocal Authenticity:** Solving the "False Yes" paradox where verbal agreement masks internal hesitation.
- **Unfiltered Reality:** Providing leadership with a diagnostic tool that bypasses the optimism bias of standard meeting reports.
- **Cognitive Resilience:** Ensuring that mission-critical intent remains verifiable even in edge-case environments (blackouts/offline).

## 3. Key Differentiators
- **Spec-Driven:** Engineered using Kiro SDD for technical precision.
- **Hardware-Anchored:** Every diagnostic is signed via NIST P-256 for institutional-grade trust.
- **Vocal-First:** Powered by ElevenLabs high-fidelity prosody analysis.

## 4. Success Definition
Success is achieved when a user can identify a high-risk commitment through a "Vocal Intelligence Briefing" before it impacts the project's bottom line.
